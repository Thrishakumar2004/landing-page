About India

India is a country in South Asia, known for its rich cultural heritage, diverse landscapes, and historical landmarks. With a population of over 1.4 billion, it is the second-most populous country in the world. India is home to ancient civilizations, vibrant traditions, and some of the most iconic architectural wonders.

Famous Places

Taj Mahal

Agra, Uttar Pradesh

A symbol of love, the Taj Mahal is one of the most famous landmarks in the world.

Qutub Minar

Delhi

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, Qutub Minar is a towering minaret that showcases ancient Islamic architecture.

Hampi

Karnataka

The ruins of Hampi are a testament to the grandeur of the Vijayanagara Empire.

Kerala Backwaters

Kerala

The backwaters of Kerala offer a serene and picturesque view of lush landscapes and tranquil waters.

Golden Temple

Amritsar, Punjab

The Golden Temple is a significant spiritual site and a stunning example of Sikh architecture.

Jaisalmer Fort

Rajasthan

Known as the Golden Fort, Jaisalmer Fort is a magnificent structure made of yellow sandstone.
